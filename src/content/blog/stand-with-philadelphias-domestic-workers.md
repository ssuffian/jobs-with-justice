This month, the PA Domestic Workers Alliance & Councilmember Quinones Sanchez will introduce a Philadelphia Domestic Worker Bill of Rights in City Council and fight to become the largest city in the country to win dignity and respect for nannies, house cleaners, and care providers! Join the PA Domestic Workers Alliance, Councilmember Maria Quinones Sanchez, Councilmember Helen Gym, other allies in Council, and supporting organizations at our press conference announcing the legislation! ![](http://phillyjwj.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/03/STAND-WITH-PHILLY-DOMESTIC-WORKERS-791x1024.png)
